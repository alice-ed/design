Project

Gaining wider input

Our partnership working is supplemented by our local authority co-design group, which is made up of a larger group of LA representatives covering various roles and levels of seniority. The group supports our digital products by bringing together the perspectives and experiences of LAs across England, providing regular input to our roadmap, designs and development work. The group have met regularly for over a year and have participated in a wide range of workshops that help to shape our products, for example in identifying new features, project risks, shaping project communications. The LA members bring their own expertise and share family hub progress in their local areas.

For the private betas, we are just testing the ability to search and find support services from a directory. We are also looking into how we can build in national level advice and guidance for families and national support services.
